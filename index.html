Horses in the US

Horses have been a major part in America's history with the Cowboys and Indians. While you would never know it today horses became extinct around 10,000 years ago. It was only when the Spanish came to America that we got these animals back. There are approximatly 9.2 million horses currently in the US. Now 4.6 million Americans are currently involved in some type of equine industry. There are almost 35,000 horses running wild in different parts of the country.
